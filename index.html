OUR STORY

Rooted In Southern Traditions

ITLU is a loving celebration of the rich, diverse vegetarian culinary history of Southern India. Rooted in the timeless warmth of traditional kitchens, our recipes have been passed down through generations—honed in grandmother's homes and regional feasts across Andhra, Telangana, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Kerala.

We combine time-honored cooking methods—like slow-simmered brass-pot pulaos, stone-ground chutneys, and freshly prepared tiffins—with modern, refined presentation to deliver a sophisticated dining experience. Every recipe is a tribute to the hands that built our culture.

THE MAJESTIC THALI

The Royal Southern Feast

RAJABHOJANAM ₹399

Our Rajabhojanam Thali is the ultimate culinary journey across the five Southern states. Served traditionally in custom brassware, it is a beautifully balanced royal meal designed to engage all your five senses.

A Curated Feast Including:

  • Ammamma Specials: Slow-cooked regional signature curries
  • Neyyi Annam & Pappu: Steaming rice served with pure ghee and regional dal
  • Sambar & Charu: Authentic, aromatic lentil soup and tangy pepper rasam
  • Traditional Breads: Hot, puffy pooris served fresh from the kadai
  • Podi & Pachadi: Hand-pounded spice powders and spicy chutneys
  • Dessert: Rich, traditional sweets like Kesari and Payasam
